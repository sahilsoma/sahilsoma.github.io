☆ ShareABook

I created this project as a major component of my master's programme. The project was created to address a significant problem that majority of household in India and various other nations experiences. The issue I'm attempting to address here has to do with handwritten notes and textbooks that aren't treated with the appropriate respect and disposed of once a student passes a class and moves on to the next one. With this effort, I aimed to increase awareness of the problem. A suitable approach that can assist in addressing the trashing of beneficial study resources, redistributing them for maximum application, and lessening the strain on natural resources was also put forth. The project was developed using Bootstrap, PHP/MariaDB, and some Stackoverflow code.
